Another Day, Another Wallet Hack

Remember that scene from classic teen movie She’s All That where the awkward, nerdy girl played by Rachel Leigh Cook takes off her glasses and suddenly she’s so attractive that no one can stop staring at her? That’s kind of what crypto feels like today. Everyone is watching.

 

But that means everyone is watching. Bad actors keep finding new ways to exploit vulnerabilities and careless actors play right into their hands. And now we’ve got the ultimate palantir gazing down upon us: the U.S. government.

 

And all we wanted to do was ditch the glasses for a nice pair of contacts.

 

It’s easy to wipe your hands of it all and say “We’re NGMI.” But easy ≠ true. Bear markets mean build markets and the Great Merge is finally becoming real. More importantly, we’re learning from our mistakes and realizing that the core values of crypto—decentralization and community—are more important than ever.

 

Some dude once said, “If you’re going through hell, keep going.”

 

See you all on the other side.
